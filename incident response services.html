Our Incident Response Process

We follow a proven methodology to rapidly contain security incidents and restore normal operations with minimal business disruption.

1

2

3

Containment & Eradication

Incident Response Case Studies

Financial Services Breach

BUSINESS EMAIL COMPROMISE

Challenge: Sophisticated BEC attack resulted in unauthorized wire transfers totaling $2.3M.

Response: Our team traced the attack vector, identified compromised accounts, and worked with financial institutions to recover 92% of funds.

Result: Full recovery of operations and implementation of advanced authentication controls.

Healthcare Ransomware

RANSOMWARE ATTACK

Challenge: Ryuk ransomware encrypted critical patient records and hospital systems, demanding $850,000.

Response: Containment within 2 hours, system restoration from backups, and negotiation reducing payment by 70%.

Result: 98% of systems restored within 48 hours with minimal patient care disruption.

ADVANCED PERSISTENT THREAT

Response: Full digital forensics investigation, threat actor attribution, and complete network remediation.

Result: Eradication of threat actor presence and implementation of advanced detection controls.
